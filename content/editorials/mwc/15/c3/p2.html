Mechanical Pencils Editorial mwc15c3p2

aurpine

Greedily take the best pencil (smallest \(K\) value), no matter what lead pack. Then, using this best pencil, try each lead pack and take the best.

Time Complexity

\(\mathcal{O}(N + MS)\), where \(N\) is the number of pencils, \(M\) is the number of packs and \(S\) is the number of pieces of lead in each pack.
