Love Guru 2 Editorial mwc15c4p1

aurpine

We are given a system of equations. Say the given variables were \(X\), \(Y\) and \(Z\). Then:

$$ X = A + B $$ $$ Y = B + C $$ $$ Z = A + C $$

We need to find the values of \(A\), \(B\) and \(C\).

$$ X + Y = A + 2B + C $$ $$ Z = A + C $$ $$ X + Y = Z + 2B $$ $$ X + Y - Z = 2B $$ $$ B = \frac {X + Y - Z}{2} $$

Now that the value of \(B\) is known, the other variables can be found.

$$ X = A + B $$ $$ A = X - B $$

And the last one...

$$ Y = B + C $$ $$ C = Y - B $$

There are other ways to solve this equation.

Time Complexity

\(\mathcal{O}(1)\)
