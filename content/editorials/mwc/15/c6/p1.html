Regpo Editorial mwc15c6p1

aurpine

The formula for interior angle of a regular polygon is \((N - 2) \times 180^\circ \div N\)

The exterior angle is \(180^\circ - \text{interior angle}\), thus \(180^\circ - (N - 2) \times 180^\circ \div N\)

Time Complexity

\(\mathcal{O} (1)\)
