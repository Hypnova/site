Lifting Weights Editorial jdcc15nova

aurpine

Mathematically find the weight she is able to lift after the number of weeks. Lily can lift \(A\) kg on week 1 (\(N = 1\)). So there's no improvement in the first week. Thus, the expression is \(A + B \times (N - 1)\).

Time Complexity

\(\mathcal{O}(1)\)
