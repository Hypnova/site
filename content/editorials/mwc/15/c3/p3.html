Bad News Editorial mwc15c3p3

atarw

Treat the grid as a maze, and simply DFS for each word to determine if it is found in the puzzle.

Time Complexity

\(\mathcal{O}(N^2Q)\), since running a DFS for each query takes \(N^2\) time on the grid in the worst case.
