Pricing Tacos mhc16g1

Samantha Unger, Sarah Goldfarb

Tiffany is making tacos. She puts a certain quantity of lettuce leaves, cheese and meat on them. Each of these ingredients have a different price. Determine how much a taco costs to produce.

Input

The first line will contain the price of a lettuce leaf, in dollars.

The second line will contain the price of a portion of cheese, in dollars.

The third line will contain the price of a portion of meat, in dollars.

The fourth line will contain the number of lettuce leaves on the taco.

The fifth line will contain the number of cheese portions on the taco.

The sixth line will contain the number of meat portions on the taco.

Output

You must output the cost of the taco. You may end up getting many decimals due to a Java error. We want 2 decimal places!

Sample Input

0.55
1.20
1.77
10
8
2

Sample Output

16.64
